AccessMap - College of Wooster

What is it?

Welcome to AccessMap, the application for reporting accessibility issues to the College of Wooster! Create an account, make a report, and keep us accountable!

Why is this necessary?

Schools are known to be inaccessible to people with disabilities. We want to create a better system for tracking access needs and respond to accessiblity concerns around campus.
